## Starter Accessories & Budget (~$250–350 beyond printer)

### Filament (Start Here)

| Type | Colors | Use Case | Notes |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| PLA Matte | Black, White, Green, Natural | Indoor items, prototypes, garden markers | Easiest to print, no heated enclosure needed |
| PETG | Black, White, Natural | Outdoor/functional parts (UV + moisture resistant) | Slightly harder to tune, keep speeds reasonable |
| PLA Basic | Assorted | Kids' projects, test prints | Cheap, forgiving |

**Brands:** Bambu Lab house filament (tuned for their printers), Polymaker PolyLite, eSun PLA+, Sunlu

**Quantity:** Start with 4–6 rolls total (2–3 PLA Matte + 1–2 PETG + 1 basic for experiments)

### Essential Accessories

| Item | Est. Cost | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Filament dryer | $40–60 | **Critical for TN humidity** — wet filament = failed prints, stringing, weak parts |
| Desiccant packs (rechargeable) | $10–15 | Keep opened rolls dry between prints |
| Extra PEI build plate (textured) | $25–35 | Swap between PLA (smooth) and PETG (textured) without re-leveling |
| IPA + microfiber cloths | $10 | Bed cleaning between prints |
| Flush cutters + scraper | $10–15 | Support removal |
| Small vacuum/brush | $10 | Keeping laser bed debris-free |

### Laser-Specific Accessories

## Fume Extraction Setup (Office Install)

The H2S has a built-in carbon filter that handles 3D printing fumes fine. **Laser work requires active exhaust venting outside.**

### Recommended Window Exhaust Setup (~$75–100)

**Chain:** H2S enclosure → 4" flex duct → inline fan → 4" flex duct → window vent panel → outside

| Component | Product | Est. Cost | Link |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------|
| Window vent panel | Flamorrow 4" Window Vent Kit | ~$25 | [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Flamorrow-Adjustable-Insulating-Enclosure-Ventilation/dp/B0DZ66LB43) |
| Window vent (heavy duty alt) | Vent Works Window Dryer Vent | ~$35 | [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Window-Vent-Adjusts-Through-Works/dp/B01M17V4CO) |
| Inline exhaust fan | AC Infinity Cloudline S4 (4") | ~$40 | Amazon |
| Flex duct | 4" aluminum flex duct (8ft) | ~$10 | Hardware store |

**How it works:** Flat panel sits in window opening, adjustable side wings expand to fill width (fits 16.5"–47"), close window on top to lock. No permanent modifications. Pull it out when not in use.

**Important:** The inline fan is essential — the H2S carbon filter alone is NOT sufficient for laser smoke/fumes. Active extraction pulls smoke out before it enters the room.

## Safety Notes

- **Never leave laser unattended** — fire risk with wood/leather materials
- **Veg-tanned leather only** — chrome-tanned leather and PVC release toxic chlorine gas
- **Air Assist is included** — keeps lines crisp by blowing away smoke/char, prevents soot buildup
- **Eye protection required** if operating laser with enclosure open
- **Keep fire extinguisher nearby** during laser operations
