Design Spec — YieldHistoryGrid.tsx (v2)

  • 4 columns per county card: Farm # | Crop | Practice | APH Yield
  • Practice chip: NI = gray background #f0f0ea / dark text  |  IRR = blue background #eff6ff / blue text #1d4ed8
  • APH Yield cell: right-aligned, amber #c05c00, bold
  • County header: dark green #2c4a3e, white text, crop count shown
  • Grid: strict 2-column (grid-cols-2), gap 16px
  • NO APH Summary table below the cards — removed from scope
  • Stripe rows: alternating #FAFAFA / white
  • Data source: APH_crop_N_{farm_number, type, county, practice, aph_yield} for N=1–24
  • Same crop with both IRR and NI practice = two separate rows
  • Crops with no data across all 5 fields are filtered out (not rendered)
