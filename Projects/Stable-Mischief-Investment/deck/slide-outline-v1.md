## GAMMA PROMPT (paste this first)

> Create a professional investor pitch deck for Stable Mischief, an agricultural technology product company raising a $7.5–$10M seed round. Use a clean, modern design with a dark background (near-black), white headline text, and a warm green accent color (#4CAF50 or similar). Font: Inter or similar sans-serif, large and bold for headlines. One idea per slide. No bullet points longer than one line. 14 slides total. Content provided below slide by slide.

## SLIDE 2 — WHAT WE DO

**SUBHEAD:** AgFin — our flagship — went from concept to production-grade platform in under 90 days.

## SLIDE 3 — THE PROBLEM

**HEADLINE:** American agriculture runs on paperwork. And it's breaking.

**SUBHEAD:** One Farm Credit association used 9 systems to originate a single loan. Another described closing packages as "an inch and a half thick."

**CONTENT:**
- $205B in farm loans processed largely by hand
- 55 Farm Credit associations — most still on legacy or homegrown systems
- The agronomist, the input retailer, the co-op — same story across the ecosystem
- More than half of ag lenders only began digitizing in 2025

**DESIGN NOTE:** Lead with the two real quotes — those are the most powerful elements. Put them large. The stats support them, not the other way around. Avoid making this feel like a market size slide — it's a pain slide.

## SLIDE 4 — WHY NOW

**HEADLINE:** Three forces converged in 2024. The window is open now.

**CONTENT:**
- **AI capability crossed the threshold** — agentic AI can now read and process complex agricultural documents with the accuracy regulated lending requires
- **Regulation created urgency** — FCA Cyber Risk Management rule (Jan 2025) requires SOC 2, AI governance, and data encryption from all Farm Credit vendors
- **The digitization wave peaked** — lenders who digitized in 2025 are discovering that digitized paperwork is still paperwork. The next layer is intelligence.

**DESIGN NOTE:** Three rows, each with an icon and a bold first clause followed by one line of explanation. Clean visual hierarchy. The regulatory point is the differentiated insight — make it stand out.

## SLIDE 5 — THE FLAGSHIP: AGFIN

**HEADLINE:** AgFin: compliance-grade document intelligence for agricultural lending.

**SUBHEAD:** Built in under 90 days. 1,395 fields. 91 passing tests. Provisional patent filed.

**DESIGN NOTE:** The pipeline flow (upload → extract → arbitrate → audit → certify) should be a simple left-to-right visual. Keep the four bullet stats tight. The $1.24 number should be called out large.

## POST-GAMMA EDITING NOTES

After Gamma generates the first draft:

1. **Typography check:** Every headline should be readable from across a room. If it requires squinting, increase the font size.
2. **One idea test:** Cover each slide and ask "what was the one thing on that slide?" If the answer isn't instant, simplify.
3. **Slide 10 table:** The comparison table may need manual cleanup in Google Slides — Gamma sometimes mangles tables.
4. **Slide 12 headshots:** Add real headshots manually after generation. Gamma placeholders won't do.
5. **Color:** The green accent should appear on key numbers and checkmarks — not on body text.
6. **Remove all bullet points** that exceed one line. If a bullet needs two lines, it's two bullets or it shouldn't be on the slide.
