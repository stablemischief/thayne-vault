## What Stable Mischief Is

An **agricultural technology product company** that uses AI-native development methodology to rapidly identify, build, validate, and deploy software products that help farmers generate and keep more revenue per acre.

The investment is NOT in any single product — it's in the **machine that builds what agriculture needs next**. AgFin is proof the methodology delivers.

**The methodology:** Identify a problem → build a working solution → validate with real users → decide whether to scale or move on — in weeks, not years. Compressed product cycle = operational moat. Capital efficiency = financial moat.

## The Three-Product Portfolio

| Product | What It Does | Pricing |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **AgFin** | Compliance-grade document intelligence for agricultural loan origination | $100/application → $50K–$750K+ ARR tiers |
| **Kowerk** | Business AI workspace for ag organizations (persistent, project-based, private model option) | $25–$100/user/month |
| **aiBA** | AI business analyst — structured discovery sessions → process docs → Kowerk knowledge base | $2,500/engagement + Kowerk upsell |

**The flywheel:**
- AgFin penetrates agricultural lenders — the institutions at the center of the ag financial ecosystem
- Kowerk reaches the broader agricultural support network — agronomists, input retailers, co-ops, farm service orgs
- aiBA maps the ecosystem's pain and feeds both the knowledge base (stickiness) and the product roadmap (direction)

Each product makes the others more valuable. Each new customer makes the data more useful. Not a collection of tools — an interconnected platform designed to compound.

**The aiBA data advantage:** Every aiBA engagement produces anonymized, aggregate intelligence about how agricultural organizations operate — where friction is, what breaks, where technology is absent. Across hundreds of engagements, those patterns reveal the highest-value problems remaining in the ecosystem. No competitor has access to this signal.

### Why Now
Agentic AI crossed the production threshold in 2024. The window is open — organizations that recognize this and build for it first will define the category. It will not stay open.

### Market Size
- **Agricultural lending:** U.S. commercial banks held $205B in farm loans (year-end 2024). 300,000+ new ag loans originated per year. 50%+ of ag lenders only began digitizing in 2025.
- **Farm Credit System:** 55 associations, $456.9B in outstanding loans, 600,000+ borrowers, $7.975B net income 2025. Actively consolidating (69 → 55 associations 2019→2024; headed toward ~20). Every merger = technology rationalization window.
- **Crop insurance:** 12 Approved Insurance Providers, 2.1M policies/year, $192B covered liability. Same FSA-578s AgFin already processes.
- **SBA lending:** 84,400 loans totaling $44.8B (FY2025) — natural expansion once ag lending foundation is established.

### Competitive Positioning
- **nCino** — workflow platform (moves loans through stages). AgFin is the document intelligence layer underneath. Complementary, not competing.
- **Abrigo / Baker Hill + FINPACK** — own credit analysis in community/regional banks. AgFin is the extraction/auditability layer underneath them.
- **Landjourney Technologies** — raised $4.6M seed in 2025, already raising Series A within their first year. Confirms institutional capital is moving in. Their hiring confirms they're building workflow-first, document intelligence second. AgFin is already where they're trying to go.
- **No competitor** currently combines parallel multi-agent extraction, field-level arbitration, analyst review workflow, and an immutable compliance audit trail in a single ag-focused platform.

### AgFin Revenue Model (three stages)
1. **Per-application:** $100/application (mirrors Ocrolus pricing model)
   - Community banks (<500 loans/yr): ~$50K–$100K ARR
   - Regional banks (500–3,000 loans/yr): ~$120K–$300K ARR
   - Farm Credit enterprises: $250K–$750K+ ARR
2. **Annual SaaS licensing:** As AgFin deepens into full credit solution
3. **Ag lending marketplace (month 24):** Farmers upload once, receive competing lender offers — origination-based revenue at fundamentally different scale

## Three Patentable Innovations (Provisional Filings In Progress)

1. **Dual-Model Consensus Extraction with Selective Arbitration** — Two AI models from independent families extract in parallel. Domain-aware comparison layer identifies genuine disagreements vs cosmetic differences. Only disputed fields (typically 5–20%) escalated to third model for arbitration. Novel approach vs single-model or simple ensemble voting.

2. **Confidence-Weighted Extraction with Mandatory Human Triage Gating** — Multi-factor confidence score computed per field. Fields below threshold require human review before certification is permitted. Hard architectural constraint — not a dashboard metric.

3. **Immutable Audit Trail with Cryptographic Hash Chain for Lending Workflows** — Three independent tamper-evidence mechanisms (DB triggers blocking modification + no-modification API surface + SHA-256 hash chain) combined with automatic PII encryption before audit storage and mandatory justification codes for every edit.
